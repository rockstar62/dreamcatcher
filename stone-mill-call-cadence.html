7 Attempts, No Answer
Mark contact "Dormant" in HubSpot
Set 60-day revisit task — market conditions change
Keep on monthly email nurture — passive touch
Not a loss — a pause. Projects come online. Revisit every 60 days
1
Always log before you move on
Every call gets logged in HubSpot — even hang-ups. If it's not logged, it didn't happen.
2
Max 3 voicemails per sequence
Days 1, 5, and 10. All other attempts are silent. Too many voicemails = annoying. Three is the Blount sweet spot.
3
Email always follows a voicemail
Same day as every voicemail. The combo is the system — one without the other is half the punch.
4
Never read from the script
Know the beats. Talk like a builder talking to a builder. "Expensive, maybe. But shouldn't forever be worth more?"
5
Dormant is not dead
7 attempts with no answer = 60-day pause, not a delete. Market conditions change. Projects come online.
6
Research before you dial
5 minutes on their website before call #1. Know one project. Know their style. Builder-to-builder means you've done your homework.
