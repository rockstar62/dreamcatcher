The Stone Mill Cold Call Script
Introductory · Awareness · Seed Planting
🎯
Goal of this call: Awareness and introduction only. Get the email. Send the brochure live. Make her the hero who found the local source. Do not sell stone.
Primary Script
Original Opener
Voicemail
Your Opener
Deliver this — then stop and listen
"Good morning Jennifer, this is Steve with The Stone Mill. There's a Pure Michigan option for your stone needs and most builders don't know it exists — it's right in your backyard.

I'm playing the long game here — I'm looking for a collaborator on the inside who can help me spread the word.

Would you be opposed to me sending you a digital brochure to review?"
Hook first. Pure Michigan does the emotional work. Long game sets you apart from every other salesperson she's heard today.
⏸ She says "No" — meaning Yes
Let it land. Then go to the second ask.
Second Ask
"The emails that get forwarded from someone trusted actually get opened — would you be opposed to passing it along to whoever handles stone selections while we're on the phone?"
Two asks. Two natural pauses. She's in the driver's seat both times. Chris Voss: "Would you be opposed" invites a no that means yes.
When They Respond
You say
"Perfect — what's the best email to send it to?"

[Get the email. Send it immediately.]

"I just sent it — do you mind pulling it up real quick? Just want to make sure it came through."
Then
Walk her through it briefly while she has it open. Ask who else on the team would want to see it. She's now the person who found the local Michigan stone source.
You say
"A local source?"

[Pause. Let it land.]

"I'm not asking you to change anything — would you be opposed to keeping our brochure on file, just in case?"
Then
If she softens, get the email and send it. If she holds firm, thank her by name, log the contact, move on. The seed is planted.
You say
"Natural quarried stone — we own the quarries right here in Michigan. Everything from fieldstone to ledge to boulders. It's all in the brochure — would you be opposed to me sending it over right now?"
Then
Curiosity is an open door. This is your best outcome. Get the email and send it live.
You say
"I appreciate that — before you do, would you be opposed to grabbing a copy of the brochure for yourself? I'd love for you to have it too."
Then
Get her email before the transfer. If you reach the designer, deliver the opener fresh. Two seeds planted on one call.
You say
"That's really helpful, I appreciate it. Would you be opposed to me sending a brochure directly to you so you have something to pass along? Makes it easy."
Then
You're making her job easier. She's likely to say yes. Get the email — now she's your internal advocate.
When She Gives You The Email
1
Send the brochure immediately — while she's still on the phone. Not later.
2
"I just sent it" — ask her to pull it up to confirm it came through.
3
Walk her through it briefly — one or two things to notice. Keep it short.
4
Log in HubSpot immediately after the call — name, email, outcome, next step.
Original Opener
Version 1 — Clean and Direct
"Good morning, this is Steve with The Stone Mill. I'm reaching out to spread the word that your stone doesn't have to come from six hundred miles away — we produce it right here in Michigan. I could really use your help — would you be opposed to me sending a brochure over to you right now while I have you on the phone?"
Shorter and faster. Good for high volume days when you need to move quick. Chris Voss "would you be opposed" framing intact.
Voicemail Script
🎙 Leave This — Then Send The Email
"Good morning Jennifer, this is Steve with The Stone Mill. I know you're busy so I'll be brief. There's a Pure Michigan option for your stone needs and most builders don't know it exists — it's right in your backyard. I'm going to follow up with a short email with a digital brochure — I'd love for you to take a look and see if it passes the test. Again, Steve Miller, The Stone Mill, [number]. Have a great day."
Blount sequence: voicemail plants the name, email reinforces it. By the time you call again you're not a stranger. Send the brochure email immediately after hanging up.
